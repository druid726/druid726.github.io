Week 1 of Phase 0

Three Websites

17 May 2014

Websites, websites, websites. So many to choose from which shall it be?? I'll go with three I look at every day.

IMDb - Internet Movie Database. Love this site, there is a good mixture of vertical and horizontal elements with great flow. Access to information is right at your fingertips. It's a great combination of a database with UI and UX.

When describing the site I would say it's:

  • organized
  • elegant
  • polished

Ruby Lane - This site sells antiques and art work. I like the gallery style layout with the items in frames. A lot of information can be gathered by simply rolling over the image. The one aspect I don't like about it the search feature. I think it should up in the header by the navbar.

When describing the site I would say it's:

  • striking
  • bold
  • elegant
  • simple

Etsy - I love handmade things. I particularly like the Etsy site because you get to interact the artisans and craftsman. Visually this is a very calm, soothing site. I almost feel like I am in a small town coffee shop discussing their latest works.

  • relaxing
  • welcoming
  • elegant
  • simple
