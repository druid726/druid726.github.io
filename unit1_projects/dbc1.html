How the hell did I get here?

I like scenic routes which is good because that is all I seem to take. No 'the shortest distance between two points is a straight line' for me!! I have a BA in Art History (no Obama jokes please) and was fortunate enough to work for the Museum of Modern Art. Which was awesome!! I had the opportunity to meet aritists I had read about and see their work up close. Unfortunately I did not have the opportunity to eat. The pay was on the low scale -- like shoe budget was higher the annual pay low.

I jumped ship and landed in Wall Street/Corporate America wasteland working as a Secretary/Word Processor/Desktop Publisher. Icky. After several years I become frustrated and bored and was actually relieved to be laid-off. So I spent a lot of free time playing on the computer and taking on-line courses in web design. I finally bit the bullet and got serious about making this career change and dedicate myself to becoming a developer. I didn't find DBC, DBC sort of found me. I meet a graduate of the Chicago program a few months ago at a NYC Ruby Women meet-up and she sold me on the idea. And that's the abridged story of how I got here.
